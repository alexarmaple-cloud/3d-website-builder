Why People's Coffee

Where Community Brews

At The People's Coffee, great coffee is just the beginning. We built a space worth returning to.

☕

Skillfully Crafted Drinks

Every cup prepared with care and consistency by our trained baristas — from your classic latte to ceremonial matcha.

🥑

Fresh Food, Every Morning

Locally sourced ingredients, house-made spreads, and daily pastries. Whether it's an avocado toast or a lox bagel, we've got your morning covered.

Heart of Downtown SLC

Located at 177 E 200 S — steps from everything. Indoors with city views or out on the patio, it's your spot.
